MASTER BEDROOM

A private structural sanctuary fully optimized for absolute rest and tranquil isolation.

The Floating Sanctuary

The bed is positioned on a custom architectural platform that seemingly cantilevers off the floor plane. Upholstered in muted, charcoal textile layers, the sleeping zone is framed by dark slate wall panels that absorb sound reflection to maintain a quiet, calm internal environment.

Unbounded Views

The entire forward structural facade is formed of floor-to-ceiling glass panel arrays, allowing the bedroom to look straight over the dark infinity lines outside. Automated perimeter shades drop completely from concealed ceiling slits, transitioning the room from transparent outdoor unity to an absolute dark concrete vault.
